Does This Sound Familiar?

First steps. First day of school. Birthdays. You saw them on video call. And you told yourself it was for them — but you both know the cost.

A source of income that doesn't require you to leave your family.

To come home — for good — on your timeline, not your employer's.

Not because your contract ended. Because you chose to. Because you had something waiting for you.

Not dependent on your body showing up to work. Not one flight cancellation away from crisis.

Money working for you while you sleep, not just when you work.

An income that grows even on rest days. Something you built, not just something you were paid for.
