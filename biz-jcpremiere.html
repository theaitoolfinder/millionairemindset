Sound Familiar?

Working abroad is noble work. But it comes with real financial and lifestyle challenges that most people don't talk about openly.

01

Your family depends on your remittances. But what happens if you get sick, your contract ends, or you simply want to come home? One income stream is a risk you can't afford.

Traditional businesses need you to be there — a store, an office, a warehouse. You're thousands of miles away. Most business models weren't built for people like you.
