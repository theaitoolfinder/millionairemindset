My Story

From OFW to Financial Educator

Like many of you, I left home with one goal: to give my family a better life. I worked hard, sent money home every month, and did everything I was supposed to do. But after years of working abroad, I realized that the money I was sending was just being spent — and I had no real plan for my future.

That wake-up call changed everything. I started learning about financial literacy, investing, insurance, and business. I joined IMG to understand money and protection, JC Premiere to create extra income while helping people with their health, and Vista Land to help fellow OFWs invest in the one dream we all share — our own home in the Philippines.

Now I run Millionaire Mindset to share everything I've learned — so you don't have to figure it out the hard way like I did.

My Mission & Values
