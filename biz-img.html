Income Streams

Three Ways You Get Paid as an IMG Advisor

First-Year Commission

Earn a percentage of the annual premium on each new policy you sell. This is your direct reward for helping a new family get covered.

2

Renewal Commission

Every year that a client renews their policy, you earn again. This builds a growing base of passive, recurring income over time.

As you grow your team, you earn a percentage of your team members' production. The bigger your team, the more you earn from their work.

Getting Started

Three Steps to Become an IMG Financial Advisor

The process is straightforward and the training is provided. You do not need a finance degree to begin.

1

Attend a Free Financial Literacy Seminar

Your first step is learning, not selling. Attend an IMG orientation or seminar to understand the financial planning framework and the business model.

2

Complete the IC Licensing Exam

To sell insurance products legally, you must pass the Insurance Commission licensing exam. IMG provides the training and materials to prepare you.

3

Start Advising and Building Your Team

Once licensed, begin conducting financial reviews with family and friends. Start building your client base and recruiting your own team of advisors.
