Business Ideas for OFWs
  • Online ResellingStart a small online shop selling in-demand goods to family and friends back home — low capital, manageable from abroad.
  • Sari-Sari Store FranchiseFund a family member to run a neighborhood store — a common first business for OFW families building passive income.
  • Real Estate InvestmentPre-selling condos or house-and-lot packages are popular among OFWs looking to grow long-term wealth in the Philippines.
  • Freelancing & Remote SkillsWriting, design, bookkeeping, or virtual assistance can become a second income stream that keeps earning after you return home.
  • Food & Catering BusinessFilipino food businesses — from small eateries to catering services — remain one of the most common OFW-funded ventures.
